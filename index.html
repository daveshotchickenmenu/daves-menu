Dave's Hot Chicken Menu 2026: Full Guide to Prices, Spice Levels & Best Items

If you've been searching for the complete Dave's Hot Chicken menu — with prices, spice levels, combos, and ordering tips — you've landed in the right place. Whether you're a first-timer trying to figure out what to order or a returning fan checking the latest 2026 prices, this guide covers everything you need to know before you walk through the door (or open the app).

Dave's Hot Chicken started in a Los Angeles parking lot in 2017 and has since grown into one of the fastest-growing Nashville-style hot chicken chains in the United States. The concept is simple: hand-breaded, fresh chicken — served your way, at the spice level you choose. Let's break down the full menu.

What's on the Dave's Hot Chicken Menu?

The Dave's Hot Chicken menu is refreshingly focused. You won't find a bloated list of items here. Instead, the restaurant keeps things tight and consistent — sliders, tenders, sides, shakes, and drinks. Here's a full breakdown:

Dave's Sliders

Sliders are arguably the most iconic item on the Dave's Hot Chicken menu. Each slider is made with crispy, juicy chicken served on a soft potato bun with pickles and Dave's signature sauce.

  • 1 Slider — ~$5.99
  • 2 Sliders — ~$10.99
  • 3 Sliders — ~$14.99

Each slider can be customized to any of the seven spice levels (more on that below). The 2 Sliders with Fries combo is one of the most popular orders, coming in at around $14.99 and offering excellent value.

Dave's Tenders

Tenders are the second pillar of the Dave's Hot Chicken menu. Hand-breaded and made fresh, these tenders are juicy on the inside and perfectly crispy on the outside.

  • 1 Tender — ~$2.49–$3.50
  • 3 Tenders Combo — ~$12.99
  • 5 Tenders Combo — ~$16.99

The 3-Piece Tender Combo includes tenders, a side, and a drink — making it a solid pick for a full meal. Single tenders are also available if you just want a quick, satisfying snack.

Combo Meals

Combos are where Dave's Hot Chicken delivers the best value. Every combo includes fries and Dave's signature sauce, with options to add a drink.

Combo Price

Sides

Dave's keeps the sides simple but satisfying. Every side is designed to complement the bold flavors of the chicken.

  • Crinkle-Cut Fries — crispy, golden, and addictive
  • Cheese Fries — topped with creamy melted cheese
  • Mac & Cheese — rich and creamy comfort food
  • Kale Slaw — a fresh, slightly sweet, vinegar-forward coleslaw that actually cuts through the heat of the chicken

The kale slaw is a standout. Unlike the soggy coleslaw you find at other chains, Dave's uses a fresh kale base — slightly sweet and vinegar-forward, which works beautifully against the spice of the chicken.

Cauliflower (Vegetarian) Options

Dave's Hot Chicken has a strong vegetarian lineup that's worth noting. The cauliflower is breaded and fried in the same style as the chicken — same crunch, same spice options, just no meat.

  • Cauli Slider
  • Cauli Bites
  • Top-Loaded Cauli Fries

Every cauliflower item is available at all seven spice levels, including Reaper. Keep in mind that most items still contain milk and eggs, so they are not fully vegan.

Shakes & Drinks

Dave's menu includes a selection of Top-Loaded Shakes (around 700–950 calories), Slushers, and regular soft drinks. These pair well with the spicier heat levels — having something sweet and cold on hand is never a bad idea when you're hitting Hot or above.

Dave's Hot Chicken Spice Levels Explained

One of the biggest reasons people search for the Dave's Hot Chicken menu is the famous spice levels. There are seven heat levels in 2026, and the best part — choosing your spice level costs nothing extra.

Here's the full heat scale:

Level Description
No Spice Zero heat. Full flavor. Great for kids.
Lite Mild A whisper of warmth. Very beginner-friendly.
Mild Noticeable but comfortable. Good starting point.
Medium A real kick. Most popular level for regulars.
Hot Serious heat. You'll feel it.
Extra Hot Not for the faint-hearted.
Reaper The hottest level. A waiver may be required.

Best for beginners: Start with Mild or Medium. Medium is the most commonly chosen heat level and gives you a great sense of what Dave's is all about without overwhelming your palate.

Best for heat lovers: Extra Hot and Reaper deliver genuine intensity. If you're going for Reaper, make sure you have a shake ready.

Feed a Crowd — Group Meal Options

Planning a group order? Dave's Hot Chicken has dedicated "Feed a Crowd" options designed for office lunches, game days, and family meals. The Hot Box combo at around $29.99 is the most popular group option, giving you a larger quantity of tenders and sides in one convenient order.

Is Dave's Hot Chicken Halal?

Yes — many Dave's Hot Chicken locations serve halal-certified chicken, which makes it a popular choice for a wide range of customers. It's always worth checking with your specific location to confirm halal availability, as it can vary.
